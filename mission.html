Our Mission

Transforming how the world thinks about plant-based food by making it irresistibly delicious, culturally diverse, and accessible to everyone.

Our Mission Statement

At Broccobae, our mission is to transform how the world thinks about plant-based food by making it irresistibly delicious, culturally diverse, and accessible to everyone.

We believe that small, joyful changes in how we eat can collectively create enormous positive impact for our health, animal welfare, and the planet. That's why we're committed to:

  • Creating and curating recipes that prioritize flavor and satisfaction
  • Celebrating plant-based traditions from cultures around the world
  • Building tools that make plant-based eating practical for everyday life
  • Fostering a supportive, inclusive community where everyone is welcome
  • Promoting sustainability through our content and business practices

Our Mission Pillars

These five pillars guide everything we do at Broccobae, from recipe development to community building.

Delicious Food First

We believe plant-based eating should be a joy, not a sacrifice. Every recipe we share is designed to delight the taste buds and satisfy cravings, proving that plant-based food can be the most delicious choice on the table.

Cultural Celebration

We honor the rich diversity of plant-based culinary traditions from around the world. By showcasing global cuisines and their plant-based foundations, we help preserve cultural heritage while inspiring culinary creativity.

Inclusive Community

We welcome everyone on their plant-based journey, whether they're vegan, flexitarian, or simply curious. By creating a judgment-free space, we foster connection and support among food lovers of all backgrounds.

Practical Solutions

We develop tools and resources that make plant-based eating convenient and sustainable in real life. From meal planning to shopping guides, we help people overcome barriers to incorporating more plants into their diet.

Environmental Impact

We're committed to promoting sustainable food choices that benefit our planet. Through education and advocacy, we highlight how plant-based eating can help address climate change, reduce pollution, and preserve biodiversity.

Our Impact Goals

By 2030, we aim to:

  • Inspire 10 million people to incorporate more plant-based meals into their diet
  • Reduce carbon emissions by helping our community shift to more climate-friendly eating habits
  • Preserve culinary traditions by documenting and sharing plant-based recipes from 100+ cultures
  • Support sustainable agriculture by promoting seasonal, local, and organic ingredients
  • Foster food equity by creating resources that make plant-based eating accessible across socioeconomic backgrounds

We track our progress toward these goals annually and share transparent reports with our community.
