Meet Our Founder

Yuki Tanaka

Founder & CEO

Born and raised in Tokyo, Yuki grew up in a family that valued traditional Japanese cuisine. After moving to the United States for culinary school, she discovered her passion for plant-based cooking while trying to recreate her favorite childhood dishes without animal products.

Frustrated by the lack of resources that honored both her Japanese heritage and her newfound plant-based lifestyle, Yuki started documenting her kitchen experiments on a blog in 2020. What began as a personal project quickly resonated with thousands of others seeking delicious, culturally diverse plant-based recipes.

"I believe that plant-based eating should never feel like a sacrifice. It should be a joyful exploration of flavors, textures, and traditions from around the world. That's the spirit I wanted to capture with Broccobae."

With a background in both culinary arts and digital marketing, Yuki has grown Broccobae from a simple blog into a comprehensive platform that serves millions of food enthusiasts worldwide.

Broccobae secures seed funding from sustainable food investors to expand beyond a blog into a comprehensive recipe platform with enhanced features.

The first version of our meal planning tool is released, allowing users to create customized weekly meal plans based on dietary preferences and cooking time.

Broccobae launches community forums, creating spaces for users to share their own recipes, modifications, and plant-based journey stories.

March 2022

One Million Users

Broccobae celebrates reaching one million registered users across 120 countries, demonstrating the global appeal of accessible plant-based cooking.

September 2022

The platform expands to include comprehensive cuisine guides, highlighting plant-based traditions and techniques from cultures around the world.

Launch of the Broccobae Marketplace, featuring curated sustainable kitchen tools, pantry staples, and plant-based products from ethical brands.

July 2023

Introduction of carbon footprint calculations for recipes and a commitment to plant 1 million trees through partnerships with environmental organizations.

January 2024

Global Expansion

Broccobae expands its team to include culinary experts from five continents, ensuring authentic representation of diverse food traditions.
