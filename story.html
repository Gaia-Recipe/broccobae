Meet Our Founder

Abhijeet Anand

Founder & CEO

Abhijeet Anand, an Indian data scientist working in Japan, discovered the power of plant-based eating through his daily routine. Living in Japan and maintaining a consistent vegan diet, he realized the challenges many face in finding convenient and delicious vegan options.

Driven by his passion for technology and sustainable living, Abhijeet founded Broccobae to create a comprehensive platform that helps vegan enthusiasts discover recipes, plan meals, and connect with like-minded individuals around the world.

"Broccobae was born from the simple idea that plant-based living should be accessible, enjoyable, and community-driven. Technology can bridge the gap between vegan curiosity and vegan lifestyle."

Jolina Javier

Co-Founder

Jolina Javier, a Filipina working in Japan's hospitality industry, transitioned to veganism and discovered the need for convenient plant-based dining solutions. Her experience in hospitality and passion for vegan living brings a unique perspective to making plant-based eating more accessible and enjoyable for everyone.
