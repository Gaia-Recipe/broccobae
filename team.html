Meet Our Team

We're a diverse group of food lovers, tech enthusiasts, and sustainability advocates working together to make plant-based eating delightful and accessible for everyone.

Leadership Team

Meet the visionaries guiding Broccobae's mission and growth.

Yuki Tanaka

Founder & CEO

Former chef turned tech entrepreneur with a passion for making plant-based eating accessible to all. Yuki founded Broccobae after seeing how difficult it was for people to maintain a plant-based diet without sacrificing flavor or convenience.

Carlos Rodriguez

Chief Technology Officer

Tech innovator focused on creating intuitive tools that make plant-based living easier. Carlos leads our engineering team and is passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems in food sustainability.

Aisha Patel

Community Manager

Social media expert and long-time vegan who loves building connections between food enthusiasts. Aisha oversees our thriving online community and ensures that every member feels welcome and supported.

Culinary Team

The creative minds behind our delicious recipes and culinary innovations.

Marcus Chen

Head of Recipe Development

Culinary school graduate with expertise in transforming classic dishes into plant-based masterpieces. Marcus leads our recipe development team and ensures that every dish we share is both delicious and accessible.

Sofia Martinez

Global Cuisine Specialist

Trained chef with a background in international cuisines who specializes in authentic plant-based adaptations of traditional dishes from around the world. Sofia ensures our global recipes are both authentic and accessible.

James Wilson

Nutrition Specialist

Registered dietitian with a focus on plant-based nutrition who ensures our recipes are not only delicious but also nutritionally balanced. James helps our users understand how to meet their nutritional needs through plant-based eating.

Technology Team

The innovative minds building the digital tools that power your Broccobae experience.

Priya Sharma

Lead Developer

Full-stack developer with a passion for creating intuitive, accessible user experiences. Priya leads our development team and is committed to building tools that make plant-based eating easier for everyone.

David Kim

UX/UI Designer

User experience specialist with a background in both design and psychology. David ensures that our platform is not only beautiful but also intuitive and accessible for users of all technical abilities.

Olivia Jackson

Data Scientist

AI and machine learning specialist who helps personalize the Broccobae experience for each user. Olivia works on our recommendation algorithms to help users discover recipes they'll love based on their preferences and past behavior.

United by Shared Values

Despite our diverse backgrounds and expertise, our team is united by a common set of values that guide everything we do at Broccobae.
