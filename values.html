Our Values

The core principles that guide everything we do at Broccobae, from recipe development to community building.

Our Guiding Principles

At Broccobae, our values aren't just words on a page—they're the principles that guide every decision we make, from the recipes we develop to the partnerships we form. These six core values reflect our commitment to making plant-based eating delicious, accessible, and joyful for everyone while promoting sustainability and cultural appreciation.

Deliciousness First

We believe plant-based food should be chosen for its flavor, not just its ethics. Every recipe we share is designed to delight the taste buds and satisfy cravings, proving that plant-based eating can be the most delicious choice on the table.

  • We test each recipe multiple times to ensure it delivers on flavor, texture, and satisfaction
  • We prioritize techniques that maximize the natural flavors of plant ingredients
  • We're not afraid to recreate comfort foods and indulgent treats in plant-based forms
  • We value user feedback and continuously improve our recipes based on community input
  • We believe that if food doesn't taste good, it doesn't matter how healthy or ethical it is—people won't eat it

Cultural Appreciation

We celebrate the rich diversity of plant-based culinary traditions from around the world, honoring their origins and authentic flavors while recognizing that many cultures have long histories of plant-forward cooking.

  • We research the cultural context and history behind traditional recipes
  • We collaborate with chefs and food experts from diverse backgrounds
  • We properly credit the cultural origins of dishes and techniques
  • We avoid appropriation by approaching global cuisines with respect and authenticity
  • We highlight indigenous and traditional plant-based cooking methods that have existed for generations

Inclusive Community

We welcome everyone on their plant-based journey, whether they're vegan, flexitarian, or simply curious about adding more plants to their diet. We believe in progress over perfection and meeting people wherever they are.

  • We create a judgment-free space where all questions and perspectives are welcome
  • We provide options for different dietary needs, skill levels, and equipment access
  • We celebrate small steps toward more plant-based eating rather than demanding perfection
  • We design our platform to be accessible to users with different abilities
  • We moderate our community spaces to ensure they remain supportive and respectful

Environmental Stewardship

We're committed to promoting sustainable food choices and implementing eco-friendly practices throughout our business, recognizing the profound impact our food system has on the planet.

  • We highlight seasonal and locally-adaptable ingredients in our recipes
  • We provide information about the environmental impact of different food choices
  • We partner with organizations working to create a more sustainable food system
  • We implement sustainable practices in our own operations, from green hosting to carbon offsetting
  • We promote food waste reduction through storage tips, preservation techniques, and creative leftover recipes

Continuous Innovation

We constantly explore new ways to make plant-based eating more delicious, convenient, and accessible through technology and creativity, pushing boundaries and challenging conventions.

  • We invest in research and development to create innovative tools and features
  • We stay current with emerging food technologies and ingredients
  • We experiment with new cooking techniques and flavor combinations
  • We regularly update our platform based on user feedback and changing needs
  • We collaborate with food scientists, chefs, and technologists to pioneer new approaches

Transparency

We're honest about our ingredients, processes, and partnerships, building trust through clear communication and ethical practices. We believe in being forthright about both our successes and our areas for improvement.

  • We clearly disclose sponsored content and affiliate relationships
  • We provide detailed information about ingredients and their sources
  • We share our business practices and decision-making processes with our community
  • We acknowledge when we make mistakes and explain how we're addressing them
  • We're open about the challenges of plant-based eating and don't make exaggerated claims
